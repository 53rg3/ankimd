\### tad

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/tad)

- a small or insignificant amount or degree : bit
    - might give him some water and a tad to eat
    - — C. T. Walker
- a small child, boy
- somewhat, rather
    - looked a tad bigger than me
    - — Larry Hodgson
