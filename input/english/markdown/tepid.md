\### tepid

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/tepid)

- moderately warm : lukewarm
    - a tepid bath
- lacking in passion, force, or zest
    - tepid poetry
- marked by an absence of enthusiasm or conviction
    - a tepid interest
    - a tepid response
