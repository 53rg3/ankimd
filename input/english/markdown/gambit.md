\### gambit

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/gambit)

- a chess opening in which a player risks one or more pawns or a minor piece to gain an advantage in position
- a remark intended to start a conversation or make a telling point, topic
- a calculated move : stratagem
