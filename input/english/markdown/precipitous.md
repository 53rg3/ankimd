\### precipitous

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/precipitous)

- precipitate sense 2
- very steep, perpendicular, or overhanging in rise or fall
    - a precipitous slope
- having precipitous sides
    - a precipitous gorge
- having a very steep ascent
    - a precipitous street
