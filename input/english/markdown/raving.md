\### raving

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/raving)

- irrational, incoherent, wild, or extravagant utterance or declamation
- talking wildly or irrationally
    - a raving lunatic
- ravishing
    - a raving beauty
