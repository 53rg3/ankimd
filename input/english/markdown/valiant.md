\### valiant

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/valiant)

- possessing or acting with bravery or boldness : courageous
    - valiant soldiers
- marked by, exhibiting, or carried out with courage or determination : heroic
    - valiant feats
- a valiant person
