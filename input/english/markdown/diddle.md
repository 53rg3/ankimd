\### diddle

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/diddle)

- to move with short rapid motions
- to waste (time) in trifling
- hoax, swindle
- to copulate with
- dawdle, fool
- fiddle, toy
    - diddled with the machine until it broke
