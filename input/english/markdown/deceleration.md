\### deceleration

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/deceleration)

- to reduce the speed of : slow down
    - decelerate a car
- to decrease the rate of progress of
    - decelerate growth
    - decelerate soil erosion
- to move at decreasing speed
