\### amid

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/amid)

- in or into the middle of : surrounded by : among
    - amid the crowd
- during
    - amid the fighting
- with the accompaniment of
    - resigned amid rumors of misconduct
