\### sophomoric

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/sophomoric)

- conceited and overconfident of knowledge but poorly informed and immature
    - a sophomoric argument
- lacking in maturity, taste, or judgment
    - sophomoric humor
