\### vast

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/vast)

- very great in size, amount, degree, intensity, or especially in extent or range
    - vast knowledge
    - a vast expanse
- a boundless space
    - the vast of heaven
    - — John Milton
