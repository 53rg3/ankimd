\### famine

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/famine)

- an extreme scarcity of food
    - The famine affected most of the country.
- starvation
- a ravenous appetite
- a great shortage
    - Transportation problems resulted in a coal famine.
