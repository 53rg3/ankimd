\### mutinous

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/mutinous)

- disposed to or being in a state of mutiny : rebellious
    - a mutinous crew
- turbulent, unruly
- of, relating to, or constituting mutiny
    - mutinous threats
