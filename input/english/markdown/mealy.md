\### mealy

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/mealy)

- containing meal : farinaceous
- soft, dry, and friable
- covered with meal or with fine granules
- flecked with another color
- spotty, uneven
- pallid, blanched
    - a mealy complexion
- mealymouthed
