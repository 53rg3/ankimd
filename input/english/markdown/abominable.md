\### abominable

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/abominable)

- worthy of or causing disgust or hatred : detestable
    - the abominable treatment of the poor
    - an abominable crime
- very bad or unpleasant
    - abominable weather
