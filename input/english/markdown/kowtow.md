\### kowtow

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/kowtow)

- to show obsequious deference : fawn
    - kowtows to the boss
- to kneel and touch the forehead to the ground in token of homage, worship, or deep respect
- an act of kowtowing
