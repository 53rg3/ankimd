\### sinewy

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/sinewy)

- full of sinews: such as
- tough, stringy
    - sinewy meat
- strong
    - sinewy arms
- marked by the strength of sinews
    - a demanding sinewy intelligence
    - — Helen Dudar
