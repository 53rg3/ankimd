\### liturgy

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/liturgy)

- a eucharistic rite
- a rite or body of rites prescribed for public worship
    - a baptismal liturgy
- a customary repertoire of ideas, phrases, or observances
