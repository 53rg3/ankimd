\### stagnant

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/stagnant)

- not flowing in a current or stream, without inflow and outflow
    - stagnant water
    - a stagnant pool
- stale
    - long disuse had made the air stagnant and foul
    - — Bram Stoker
- not advancing or developing
    - a stagnant economy
