\### strenuous

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/strenuous)

- vigorously active : energetic
- fervent, zealous
    - his most strenuous supporters
- marked by or calling for energy or stamina : arduous
    - a strenuous hike
