\### maternal

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/maternal)

- of, relating to, belonging to, or characteristic of a mother : motherly
    - maternal love
    - maternal instincts
- related through a mother
    - his maternal aunt
- inherited or derived from the female parent
    - maternal genes
