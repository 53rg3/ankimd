\### viscous

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/viscous)

- having a thick or sticky consistency : viscid
    - viscous secretions
    - viscous corn syrup
- having or characterized by a high resistance to flow
    - viscous lava
