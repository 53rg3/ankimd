\### ascetic

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/ascetic)

- practicing strict self-denial as a measure of personal and especially spiritual discipline
    - an ascetic monk
    - an ascetic diet
- austere in appearance, manner, or attitude
