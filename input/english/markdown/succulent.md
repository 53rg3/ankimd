\### succulent

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/succulent)

- full of juice : juicy
- moist and tasty : toothsome
    - a succulent meal
- having fleshy tissues that conserve moisture
- rich in interest
- a succulent plant (such as a cactus or an aloe)
