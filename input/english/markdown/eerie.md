\### eerie

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/eerie)

- so mysterious, strange, or unexpected as to send a chill up the spine, seemingly not of earthly origin
    - a coyote's eerie howl
    - the similarities were eerie
    - the flames cast an eerie glow
- affected with fright : scared
