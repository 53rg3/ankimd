\### stroll

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/stroll)

- to go from place to place in search of work or profit
    - strolling players
    - strolling musicians
- to walk in a leisurely or idle manner : ramble
- to walk at leisure along or about
