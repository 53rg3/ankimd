\### drowsiness

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/drowsiness)

- inducing or tending to induce sleep
    - drowsy music
- indolent, lethargic
    - drowsy bureaucrats
- giving the appearance of peaceful inactivity
