\### feral

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/feral)

- of, relating to, or suggestive of a wild beast
    - feral teeth
    - feral instincts
- not domesticated or cultivated : wild
    - feral animals
- having escaped from domestication and become wild
    - feral cats
