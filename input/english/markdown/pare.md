\### pare

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/pare)

- to trim off an outside, excess, or irregular part of
    - pare apples
    - paring his nails
- to diminish or reduce by or as if by paring
    - pare expenses
    - the novel was pared down to 200 pages
