\### forgo

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/forgo)

- to give up the enjoyment or advantage of : do without
    - never forwent an opportunity of honest profit
    - — R. L. Stevenson
    - decided to forgo dessert for a few days
- forsake
