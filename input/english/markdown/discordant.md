\### discordant

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/discordant)

- being at variance : disagreeing
    - discordant opinions
- quarrelsome
    - a discordant family
- relating to a discord (see discord entry 1 sense 2)
    - a discordant tone
