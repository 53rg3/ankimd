\### skittish

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/skittish)

- lively or frisky in action : capricious
- variable, fluctuating
- easily frightened : restive
    - a skittish horse
- coy, bashful
- marked by extreme caution : wary
    - skittish investors
