\### oat

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/oat)

- any of several grasses (genus Avena), a widely cultivated cereal grass (A. sativa)
- a crop or plot of the oat, the seed of an oat
- a reed instrument made of an oat straw
- to act in a newly self-confident and often self-important manner
