\### ardent

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/ardent)

- characterized by warmth of feeling typically expressed in eager zealous support or activity
    - ardent proponents of the bill
    - an ardent admirer
    - ardent fans
- fiery, hot
    - an ardent sun
- shining, glowing
    - ardent eyes
