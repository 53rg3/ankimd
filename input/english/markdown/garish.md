\### garish

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/garish)

- clothed in vivid colors
    - a garish clown
- excessively or disturbingly vivid
    - garish colors
    - garish imagery
- offensively or distressingly bright : glaring
- tastelessly showy : flashy
    - garish neon signs
