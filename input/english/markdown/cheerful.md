\### cheerful

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/cheerful)

- full of good spirits : merry
    - a cheerful host
- ungrudging
    - cheerful obedience
- conducive to cheer : likely to dispel gloom or worry
    - sunny cheerful room
