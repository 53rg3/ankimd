\### decisive

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/decisive)

- having the power or quality of deciding
    - The council president cast the decisive vote.
    - a decisive battle
- resolute, determined
    - a decisive manner
    - decisive leaders
    - a decisive editor
- unmistakable, unquestionable
    - a decisive superiority
