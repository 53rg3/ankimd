\### resort

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/resort)

- one that affords aid or refuge : resource
    - went to them as a last resort
- recourse sense 1a
    - have resort to outside help
- frequent, habitual, or general visiting
    - a place of popular resort
- persons who frequent a place : throng
- a frequently visited place : haunt, a place designed to provide recreation, entertainment, and accommodation especially to vacationers : a community or establishment whose purpose or main industry is catering to vacationers
- to go especially frequently or habitually : repair
- to have recourse
    - resort to force
- to sort again
