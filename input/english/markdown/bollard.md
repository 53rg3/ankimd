\### bollard

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/bollard)

- a post of metal or wood on a wharf around which to fasten mooring lines
- bitt sense 1
- any of a series of short posts set at intervals to delimit an area (such as a traffic island) or to exclude vehicles
