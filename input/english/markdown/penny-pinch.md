\### penny-pinch

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/penny-pinch)

- to give out money to in a niggardly manner, deprive of funds by petty economy
    - takes a sinister but fascinating kind of joy in … penny-pinching his own family
    - — James Yaffe
    - penny-pinched himself out of … millions of dollars
    - — S. N. Behrman
