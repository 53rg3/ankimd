\### arduous

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/arduous)

- hard to accomplish or achieve : difficult
    - an arduous task
    - years of arduous training
- marked by great labor or effort : strenuous
    - … a life of arduous toil.
    - — A. C. Cole
- hard to climb : steep
    - an arduous path
