\### creed

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/creed)

- a brief authoritative formula of religious belief
    - the Nicene Creed
- a set of fundamental beliefs, a guiding principle
    - Never settle for mediocrity is his creed.
    - — Jill Lieber
