\### ineffable

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/ineffable)

- incapable of being expressed in words : indescribable
    - ineffable joy
- unspeakable
    - ineffable disgust
- not to be uttered : taboo
    - the ineffable name of Jehovah
