\### sleazy

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/sleazy)

- lacking firmness of texture : flimsy
- carelessly made of inferior materials : shoddy
- marked by low character or quality
    - sleazy tabloids
- squalid, dilapidated
    - sleazy bars
