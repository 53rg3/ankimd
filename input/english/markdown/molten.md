\### molten

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/molten)

- fused or liquefied by heat : melted
    - molten lava
- having warmth or brilliance : glowing
    - the molten sunlight of warm skies
    - — T. B. Costain
- made by melting and casting
