\### vigorous

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/vigorous)

- done with vigor : carried out forcefully and energetically
    - vigorous exercises
- possessing vigor : full of physical or mental strength or active force : strong
    - a vigorous youth
    - a vigorous plant
