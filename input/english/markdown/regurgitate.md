\### regurgitate

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/regurgitate)

- to become thrown or poured back
- to throw or pour back or out from or as if from a cavity
    - regurgitate food
    - memorized facts to regurgitate on the exam
