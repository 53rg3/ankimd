\### reckoning

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/reckoning)

- the act or an instance of reckoning: such as
- account, bill
- computation
- calculation of a ship's position
- a settling of accounts
    - day of reckoning
- a summing up
