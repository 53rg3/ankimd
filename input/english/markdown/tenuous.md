\### tenuous

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/tenuous)

- having little substance or strength : flimsy, weak
    - tenuous influences
- shaky sense 2a
    - tenuous reasons
- not thick : slender
    - a tenuous rope
- not dense : rare
    - a tenuous fluid
