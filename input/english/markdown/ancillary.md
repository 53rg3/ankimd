\### ancillary

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/ancillary)

- subordinate, subsidiary
    - the main factory and its ancillary plants
- auxiliary, supplementary
    - the need for ancillary evidence
    - ancillary expenses
    - ancillary equipment
