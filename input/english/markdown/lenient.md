\### lenient

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/lenient)

- of mild and tolerant disposition or effect : not harsh, severe, or strict
    - lenient laws
    - a lenient attitude
- exerting a soothing or easing influence : relieving pain or stress
