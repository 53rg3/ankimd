\### amorous

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/amorous)

- strongly moved by love and especially sexual love
    - amorous couples
- being in love : enamored
    - amorous of the girl
- indicative of love
    - received amorous glances from her partner
- of or relating to love
    - an amorous novel
