\### shrewd

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/shrewd)

- marked by clever discerning awareness and hardheaded acumen
    - shrewd common sense
- given to wily and artful ways or dealing
    - a shrewd operator
- severe, hard
    - a shrewd knock
- sharp, piercing
    - a shrewd wind
- mischievous
- abusive, shrewish
- ominous, dangerous
