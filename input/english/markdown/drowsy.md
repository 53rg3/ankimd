\### drowsy

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/drowsy)

- ready to fall asleep
    - The pills made her drowsy.
- inducing or tending to induce sleep
    - drowsy music
- indolent, lethargic
    - drowsy bureaucrats
- giving the appearance of peaceful inactivity
    - a drowsy village
