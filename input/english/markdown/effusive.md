\### effusive

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/effusive)

- marked by the expression of great or excessive emotion or enthusiasm
    - effusive praise
- pouring freely
- characterized or formed by a nonexplosive outpouring of lava
    - effusive rocks
