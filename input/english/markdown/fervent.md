\### fervent

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/fervent)

- very hot : glowing
    - the fervent sun
- exhibiting or marked by great intensity of feeling : zealous
    - fervent prayers
    - a fervent proponent
    - fervent patriotism
