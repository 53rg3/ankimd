\### bubbly

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/bubbly)

- full of bubbles : effervescent
    - a bubbly bottle of pop
- full of or showing good spirits : lively, effusive
- resembling a bubble
    - a bubbly dome
- champagne sense 1
