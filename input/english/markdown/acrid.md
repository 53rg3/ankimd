\### acrid

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/acrid)

- sharp and harsh or unpleasantly pungent in taste or odor : irritating
    - acrid smoke
- deeply or violently bitter : acrimonious
    - an acrid denunciation
