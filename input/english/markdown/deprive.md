\### deprive

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/deprive)

- to withhold something from
    - deprived a citizen of her rights
- to remove from office
    - the Archbishop … would be deprived and sent to the Tower
    - — Edith Sitwell

    - — Shakespeare
