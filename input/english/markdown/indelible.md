\### indelible

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/indelible)

- that cannot be removed, washed away, or erased
- making marks that cannot easily be removed
    - an indelible pencil
- lasting
    - indelible memories
- unforgettable, memorable
    - an indelible performance
