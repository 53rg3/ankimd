\### flaccid

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/flaccid)

- not firm or stiff, lacking normal or youthful firmness
    - flaccid muscles
- deficient in turgor
- lacking vigor or force
    - flaccid leadership
