\### paternity

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/paternity)

- the quality or state of being a father
- origin or descent from a father
- granted to a father
    - paternity leave
    - paternity rights
- of or relating to the determination of paternity
    - a paternity suit
