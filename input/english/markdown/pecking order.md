\### pecking order

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/pecking+order)

- the basic pattern of social organization within a flock of poultry in which each bird pecks another lower in the scale without fear of retaliation and submits to pecking by one of higher rank, a dominance hierarchy in a group of social animals
- a social hierarchy
