\### recede

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/recede)

- to move back or away : withdraw
    - a receding hairline
- to slant backward
- to grow less or smaller : diminish, decrease
    - a receding deficit
- to cede back to a former possessor
