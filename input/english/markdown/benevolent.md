\### benevolent

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/benevolent)

- marked by or disposed to doing good
    - a benevolent donor
- organized for the purpose of doing good
    - a benevolent society
- marked by or suggestive of goodwill
    - benevolent smiles
