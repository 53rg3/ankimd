\### distraught

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/distraught)

- agitated with doubt or mental conflict or pain
    - distraught mourners
- mentally deranged : crazed
    - as if thou wert distraught and mad with terror
    - — William Shakespeare
