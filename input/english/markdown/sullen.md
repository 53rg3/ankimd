\### sullen

[Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/sullen)

- gloomily or resentfully silent or repressed
    - a sullen crowd
- suggesting a sullen state : lowering
    - a sullen countenance
- dull or somber in sound or color
- dismal, gloomy
    - a sullen morning
- moving sluggishly
    - a sullen river
